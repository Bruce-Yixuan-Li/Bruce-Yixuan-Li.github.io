About

My name is Yixuan Li. I was born by the Songhua River, Songyuan City, Jilin Province, China.

I studied at the School of Physical Sciences at the University of Science and Technology of China from 2018 to 2022. Throughout my four years of undergraduate studies, I had been experiencing the beauty of simplicity and universality in physics. Since 2022, I have been working at Wen Lab. We aim to investigate the biophysics and computational neuroscience of small animals like C. elegans, Drosophila, and zebrafish, and I believe that our work will inspire Artificial General Intelligence. For the future, I intend to work on invasive Brain Machine Interfaces to empower paralyzed people to walk and talk again.

In my spare time, I love running, basketball, hiking, music and exploring all the strange things in this strange world.
